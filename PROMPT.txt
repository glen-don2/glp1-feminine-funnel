## Scoring Algorithm
Track 4 block types:
- Cortisol: belly weight + stress cravings + 3pm crash
- Estrogen: hips weight + pre-period cravings
- Insulin: all-over weight + constant cravings
- Thyroid: upper body + tired all day

Diagnosis rules:
- Highest 2 scores = primary diagnosis
- Tie-breaker: Cortisol > Estrogen > Insulin > Thyroid
